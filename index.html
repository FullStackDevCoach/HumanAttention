Philosophy

Time, gravity, love. The forces that really kick ass are all invisible. t  respects these humane values starting from respecting our attention on the web.

t  is the first player in the web 3.0 space trying to monetize human attention in a simple matrix with the consensus achieved by Proof of Attention.
t  is a decentralized media platform with an innovative humane incen tive to create, curate, and propagate feature articles under curation market mechanism. All participants on t  network will be fairly reward ed for the value they co-contributed.

t  native token, TXT (t token) is the first cryptocurrency in the unit of representing value created from 1hour curated human attention in the attention markets. This breaks the boundary between intrinsic human existence (time) and a spendable, flowable social value (Engagement).
